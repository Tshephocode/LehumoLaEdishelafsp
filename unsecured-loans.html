What Are Unsecured Loans?

An unsecured loan is a loan that doesn't require any collateral or security. It's based on your creditworthiness and ability to repay. Benefits include:

  • No collateral required – your assets are safe
  • Fast approval process – often within hours
  • Fixed monthly payments – easy to budget
  • Flexible use – for any purpose
  • Build your credit score with timely payments
