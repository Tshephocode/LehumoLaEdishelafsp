What You Need to Qualify

Our requirements are designed to be accessible while ensuring responsible lending practices

Quick Eligibility Check

Answer these quick questions to see if you might qualify:

Loan Requirements Checklist

Make sure you meet these basic requirements before applying for an asset-backed loan

Basic Personal Requirements

Age Requirement

You must be 18 years or older. We require proof of age through your South African ID document.

Documents needed: Valid South African ID book or smart card

Residency Status

You must be a South African citizen or permanent resident with valid residency documentation.

Accepted: SA citizens, permanent residents with valid permits

Contact Details

You must have a valid South African mobile number and email address for communication.

Required: Active mobile number and email address

Asset Requirements

Asset Qualification Checklist

Ownership

You must be the legal owner of the asset being used as collateral. The asset must be fully paid off or have substantial equity.

Minimum Value

Assets must have a minimum value of R50,000. Higher value assets typically receive better loan terms.

Good Condition

The asset should be in reasonable condition for its age and type. Significant damage or deterioration may affect eligibility.

Clear Title

The asset must have clear legal title with no major encumbrances or disputes.

Asset-Specific Requirements:

Vehicles
  • Registered in your name
  • Roadworthy condition
  • Valid license disc
  • Under 15 years old (typically)
Luxury Items
  • Authenticity certificates
  • Good condition
  • Marketable items
  • Original documentation

Financial Requirements

Income Verification

You must have a verifiable source of income to demonstrate repayment ability. We accept various income types:

  • Salary/employment income (3 months payslips)
  • Business income (6 months bank statements)
  • Rental income (lease agreements)
  • Investment income (investment statements)

Bank Account

You must have an active South African bank account in your name for loan disbursement and repayments.

Accepted: Current/cheque accounts from recognized South African banks

Credit History

We check credit history as part of our responsible lending practices, but we work with various credit profiles.

We consider: Applicants under debt review may have additional requirements

Additional Considerations

Important Notes:
  • We conduct credit bureau checks as part of our assessment
  • Assets will be professionally valued by our experts
  • Insurance may be required for certain assets
  • Additional documentation may be requested based on specific circumstances
  • We comply with the National Credit Act and FSCA regulations
Automatic Disqualifications:
  • Active sequestration or liquidation
  • False or misleading information provided
  • Assets with disputed ownership
  • Illegal or prohibited items
  • Recent fraud convictions
Not Sure If You Qualify?

If you're unsure whether you meet all requirements, contact us anyway! We often work with applicants to find solutions that work for their specific situation. Many requirements have exceptions or alternative solutions.
