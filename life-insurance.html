Term Life Insurance

Affordable coverage for a specific period, providing a lump sum payment to your beneficiaries if you pass away during the term.

  • Coverage from R100,000 to R10 million
  • Fixed premiums for the term
  • Convertible to permanent cover
  • Terms from 5 to 30 years
  • Accelerated benefits option
