What is Property Bridging Finance?

Short-term finance designed to help you move quickly in the property market when timing is everything

The Fast Track to Property Opportunities

Property bridging finance is a short-term loan (typically 3-24 months) secured against property. It's designed to help you:

  • Buy at auction - Secure properties before traditional finance is approved
  • Bridge the gap - Between selling one property and buying another
  • Fund renovations - Before selling or refinancing
  • Development finance - For property developers needing quick capital
  • Prevent chain breaks - Keep property transactions moving smoothly

Unlike traditional banks that take weeks to approve property loans, we provide decisions within 24-48 hours and funding shortly after.
