Required Documents for Loan Application

These documents are required for all loan applications. Additional documents may be needed based on your specific asset type.

Asset-Specific Documents

Select your asset type to see specific document requirements:

Vehicle-Specific Documents

Vehicle Registration Certificate

Official registration document (NATIS) showing vehicle is registered in your name.

License Disc

Current vehicle license disc (must be valid).

Service History

Service and maintenance records (preferred but not always mandatory).

Roadworthy Certificate

If vehicle is older than 5 years, a roadworthy certificate may be required.

Luxury Asset-Specific Documents

Original Purchase Receipts

Original receipts or proof of purchase for luxury items.

Certificates of Authenticity

Official certificates, grading reports, or authentication documents.

Clear Photographs

High-quality photographs from all angles showing condition and details.

Insurance Documentation

Current insurance certificates or valuations if available.

Property-Specific Documents

Title Deed

Official title deed showing property ownership.

Municipal Account

Latest municipal account (not older than 3 months).

Rates Clearance Certificate

If property was recently purchased, rates clearance certificate.

Site/Building Plans

Approved building plans if available (not always mandatory).

Equipment-Specific Documents

Purchase Proof

Original purchase invoice or proof of ownership.

Maintenance Records

Service and maintenance history records.

Hours/Mileage Records

Current hours or mileage reading documentation.

Registration Documents

For registered equipment (trailers, specialized vehicles).

General Personal Documents

Identification Documents

  • South African ID (valid)
  • Driver's license (if applicable)
  • Passport (for non-SA citizens)
  • Marriage certificate (if applicable)

Proof of Residence

  • Utility bill (electricity, water)
  • Municipal account
  • Lease agreement
  • Bank statement with address

Must be less than 3 months old

Income & Financial Documents

For Salaried Employees

  • 3 most recent payslips
  • Employment contract
  • Letter from employer
  • Latest IRP5/IT3(a) certificate

For Self-Employed/Business Owners

  • 6 months bank statements
  • Latest financial statements
  • Company registration documents
  • Latest tax return (IT34)

For Property Income

  • Lease agreements
  • 3 months rental income bank statements
  • Proof of property ownership

For Pension/Investment Income

  • Pension/annuity statements
  • Investment statements
  • Proof of regular income

Additional & Supporting Documents

These documents may be requested in specific cases:
  • Divorce decree - if recently divorced
  • Proof of child maintenance - if applicable
  • Proof of other loans/credit - for debt consolidation
  • Business plans - for business expansion loans
  • Medical reports - for medical expense loans
  • Proof of study - for education expense loans
Document Preparation Tips:
  • Make clear copies - Ensure all copies are legible and complete
  • Organize by category - Group documents together for easy submission
  • Check dates - Ensure documents are current and not expired
  • Digital copies - Have digital versions ready for email submission
  • Missing documents? Contact us - we may accept alternatives or help you obtain missing documents
