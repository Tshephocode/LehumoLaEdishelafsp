Equipment Eligibility Criteria

Clear Ownership

Equipment must be fully owned with no existing financing or liens.

Working Condition

Equipment must be in good working order and properly maintained.

Market Value

Established resale value with available market comparables.

Age & Condition

Equipment should be within reasonable age limits and well maintained.

Documents Required

To process your equipment finance application, we'll need the following documentation:

Proof of Ownership

Purchase receipts, invoices, or registration documents showing ownership.

Service Records

Maintenance and service history demonstrating proper upkeep.

Valuation

Recent professional valuation or appraisal of the equipment.

Owner Identification

Valid SA ID/passport and proof of residence for all owners.

Equipment Photos

Clear photographs showing the equipment, serial numbers, and condition.
