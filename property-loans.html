Property Eligibility Criteria

Clear Title

Property must be free of any bonds or legal encumbrances, or have sufficient equity.

Market Value

Recent professional valuation or municipal valuation required.

Location

Properties in established areas with good market demand preferred.

Condition

Properties must be in reasonable condition and meet basic safety standards.

Documents Required

To process your property loan application, we'll need the following documentation:

Title Deed

Original title deed showing clear ownership and property registration details.

Rates & Taxes Certificate

Municipal rates and taxes clearance certificate (not older than 3 months).

Valuation Report

Professional property valuation report from a registered valuer.

Owner Identification

Valid SA ID/passport and proof of residence for all property owners.

Bond Statements

Latest bond statements if there's existing financing on the property.
