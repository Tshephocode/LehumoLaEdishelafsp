Vehicle Eligibility Requirements

Vehicle Condition

Good working condition with valid roadworthy certificate (if required).

Clear Title

Vehicle must be fully paid off with no existing financing.

Registration

Valid South African registration in your name.

Insurance

Comprehensive insurance coverage required for the loan term.

Documents Required

To process your vehicle loan application, we'll need the following documents:

Vehicle Registration Certificate

Original vehicle registration document (Natis) in your name, showing clear title with no encumbrances.

Roadworthy Certificate

Valid roadworthy certificate (required for vehicles over 3 years old).

Insurance Documents

Comprehensive insurance policy showing you as the insured party.

Owner Identification

Valid South African ID or passport and proof of residence (not older than 3 months).
