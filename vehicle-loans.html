What We Look For in a Vehicle

Vehicle Condition

Good working condition with valid roadworthiness certificate.

Clear Title

Vehicle must be fully paid off with no existing financing.

Registration

Valid South African registration in your name.

Insurance

Comprehensive insurance coverage required.

Loans Against Documents

In addition to physical vehicle loans, we also provide loans against vehicle-related documents. This option is perfect when you need cash quickly without immediate vehicle inspection.

Accepted Documents for Vehicle Loans

Vehicle Registration Certificate

Original vehicle registration document (Natis) in your name, showing clear title with no encumbrances.

Roadworthy Certificate

Valid roadworthy certificate issued within the last 12 months for vehicles over 3 years old.

Insurance Documents

Comprehensive insurance policy showing you as the insured party and the vehicle as insured asset.

Owner Identification

Valid South African ID or passport and proof of residence (not older than 3 months).

Proof of Ownership

Original purchase receipt or previous owner's registration if vehicle was purchased privately.
