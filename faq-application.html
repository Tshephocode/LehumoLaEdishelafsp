Our Simple Application Process

Follow these steps to get your loan approved quickly and efficiently

1

Initial Application

Complete our online application form or call us directly. Provide basic information about yourself, your loan needs, and the asset you want to use as security.

2

Document Submission

Submit required documents electronically. We'll guide you through exactly what's needed based on your specific situation and asset type.

3

Asset Assessment

Our experts assess your asset's value. This can be done remotely or through an in-person inspection, depending on the asset type.

4

Approval & Terms

Receive your loan approval with clear terms, interest rates, and repayment schedule. We explain everything in simple language.

5

Funding

Once you accept the terms, funds are transferred to your bank account. This often happens the same day as approval.

Documentation & Requirements

What documents do I need for a loan application? +

The documents required depend on your personal situation and the type of asset, but generally include:

Personal Documentation:
Identity Document

Valid South African ID or passport

Proof of Residence

Utility bill or bank statement not older than 3 months

Proof of Income

Recent payslips (3 months) or bank statements showing income

Asset-Specific Documentation:
Vehicle Loans

Vehicle registration papers, license disc, service history

Property Loans

Title deed, municipal account, rates clearance certificate

Luxury Items

Original purchase receipts, certificates, authentication documents

Note: Don't worry if you're missing some documents - contact us and we'll advise on acceptable alternatives. We make the process as simple as possible.

Can I apply if I don't have all the documents ready? +

Yes, you can start the application process even if you don't have all documents ready immediately. Here's how we can help:

  • Start with what you have: Begin the application with available documents
  • Document guidance: We'll provide a customized checklist of exactly what you need
  • Alternative options: We can often accept alternative documents if standard ones aren't available
  • Priority documents: We'll identify which documents are most urgent
  • Extension options: We can often give you time to gather missing documents

Common alternatives we accept:

  • For proof of residence: Lease agreement, insurance documents, or affidavit
  • For proof of income: SARS documents, accountant's letter, or business bank statements
  • For asset ownership: Sworn affidavits, previous registration documents, or purchase agreements

The most important thing is to start the conversation. Contact us even if you're unsure about documentation - we'll guide you through the process.

What if I'm self-employed or a business owner? +

We welcome self-employed individuals and business owners! We understand that your documentation needs are different from salaried employees.

Documents for self-employed applicants:

  • 6+ months of business bank statements
  • Financial statements (if available)
  • Latest tax returns or SARS ITA34
  • Proof of business registration (CIPC documents)
  • Business profile from CIPC
  • Proof of business address

What if I don't have formal financial statements?

  • We can work with bank statements showing regular income
  • We consider the nature and stability of your business
  • We look at overall financial picture rather than just formal documents
  • We understand seasonal fluctuations in certain businesses

Special considerations:

  • We understand that business income can fluctuate
  • We consider business assets as additional security
  • We can structure loans around business cash flow patterns
  • We offer business-specific loan products

Our team has extensive experience working with entrepreneurs. Contact us to discuss your specific business situation.

Approval & Timeline Questions

How long does the approval process take? +

Our approval process is designed for speed while maintaining thoroughness. Here's a typical timeline:

Standard Processing Times:

  • Initial Application: 5-15 minutes to complete
  • Preliminary Assessment: 1-4 hours for initial review
  • Document Verification: 4-24 hours once all documents are submitted
  • Asset Valuation: 24-48 hours (can be same-day for urgent cases)
  • Final Approval: Within 24 hours of valuation completion
  • Funding: Same day or next business day after approval

Total Typical Timeline: 2-3 business days from application to funding

Expedited Processing: For urgent needs, we offer:

  • Same-day approvals for complete applications
  • Priority processing for emergency situations
  • After-hours applications for urgent needs
  • 24-hour turnaround for well-prepared applications

Factors affecting processing time:

  • Completeness of documentation
  • Complexity of asset valuation
  • Your responsiveness to queries
  • Time of application submission

We pride ourselves on being significantly faster than traditional banks while maintaining rigorous standards.

What happens during the approval process? +

Our approval process is thorough yet efficient. Here's what happens step by step:

Step 1: Application Review

  • Verify application information completeness
  • Initial credit and affordability assessment
  • Identify any immediate concerns or questions

Step 2: Document Verification

  • Authenticate identity documents
  • Verify proof of residence
  • Assess income documentation
  • Check asset ownership and documentation

Step 3: Asset Valuation

  • Professional assessment of asset value
  • Market research and comparative analysis
  • Condition assessment and verification
  • Determination of loan-to-value ratio

Step 4: Risk Assessment

  • Affordability analysis based on income and expenses
  • Credit history review (with context)
  • Assessment of repayment capacity
  • Consideration of any risk factors

Step 5: Decision Making

  • Final review by credit committee
  • Determination of loan terms and conditions
  • Preparation of offer letter
  • Communication of decision to applicant

Throughout the process, we maintain communication with you and keep you updated on progress. We're transparent about any concerns or additional requirements.

Will I be notified at each stage of the process? +

Yes, we believe in transparent communication throughout the application process. Here's how we keep you informed:

Communication Methods:

  • Email Updates: Automatic notifications at key milestones
  • SMS Alerts: Important status updates via SMS
  • Phone Calls: Personal contact from your dedicated loan officer
  • Online Portal: Real-time status tracking (where available)
  • WhatsApp: Quick updates and document requests

Key Notification Points:

  • Application Received: Immediate confirmation
  • Document Requirements: Clear checklist of needed documents
  • Document Received: Confirmation when we receive your documents
  • Under Review: Notification when assessment begins
  • Additional Information Needed: Prompt request for any missing information
  • Asset Valuation Scheduled: Details of valuation arrangements
  • Approval Decision: Immediate notification of outcome
  • Funding Scheduled: Details of fund transfer timing

Your Dedicated Contact:

  • You'll be assigned a dedicated loan officer
  • Single point of contact throughout the process
  • Direct phone number and email for queries
  • Regular progress updates

Proactive Communication:

  • We don't leave you wondering about status
  • We anticipate questions and provide answers proactively
  • We're available during and after business hours for urgent matters
  • We provide realistic timelines and updates if they change

Our goal is to make the process as stress-free as possible through clear, consistent communication.

Funding & Disbursement Questions

How quickly will I receive the funds after approval? +

We pride ourselves on fast funding times. Here's what you can expect:

Standard Funding Times:

  • Same-Day Funding: If approved before 2pm on a business day
  • Next Business Day: For approvals after 2pm or for complex cases
  • Weekend Approvals: Funding on Monday (or next business day)

Factors Affecting Funding Speed:

  • Time of Approval: Approvals before 2pm typically fund same day
  • Bank Processing: Interbank transfer times (usually 2-4 hours)
  • Document Signing: Speed of returning signed agreements
  • Bank Verification: Time taken for FICA and bank verification

Urgent Funding Options:

  • Instant EFT: Available for certain banks (within minutes)
  • Same-Day Guarantee: For approved urgent applications
  • Weekend Emergency Funding: Available for genuine emergencies

Bank-Specific Information:

  • Major Banks (FNB, ABSA, Standard, Nedbank): Typically 2-4 hours
  • Capitec, TymeBank: Usually within 4 hours
  • African Bank, Investec: May take slightly longer
  • International Transfers: 1-2 business days

What Delays Funding:

  • Incomplete FICA documentation
  • Bank account verification issues
  • Missing signed agreements
  • Public holidays or bank system issues

We work with you to ensure all requirements are met quickly so you can receive funds as soon as possible.

How will the funds be paid to me? +

We offer several convenient funding options to suit your needs:

Primary Funding Methods:

  • Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT): Direct to your bank account (most common)
  • Instant EFT: Immediate transfer for supported banks
  • Same-Day Clearance: Guaranteed same-day payment for urgent cases

Bank Account Requirements:

  • Must be a South African registered bank account
  • Account must be in your name (matching ID document)
  • Account must be active and able to receive funds
  • We verify the account before making payment

Verification Process:

  • We verify account ownership through bank statements
  • We may request a bank confirmation letter
  • We perform FICA checks on the bank account
  • We confirm the account can receive the loan amount

Special Payment Arrangements:

  • Third-Party Payments: Can be arranged with proper documentation
  • Multiple Accounts: Funds can be split between accounts if needed
  • Creditor Payments: We can pay creditors directly on your behalf
  • Business Accounts: Payment to company accounts with proper verification

Payment Confirmation:

  • We provide payment proof and reference number
  • You'll receive SMS/email confirmation
  • We follow up to confirm receipt
  • We provide contact details if funds don't arrive

International Transfers:

  • Available for certain cases with proper documentation
  • Subject to exchange control regulations
  • May take 1-3 business days
  • Additional fees may apply

We ensure secure, traceable payments to give you peace of mind.

Are there any fees or charges for the application? +

We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Here's our fee structure:

No Upfront Application Fees:

  • Application Submission: Free
  • Initial Assessment: Free
  • Document Review: Free
  • Preliminary Approval: Free

Potential Fees (Only if Proceeding):

  • Valuation Fee: Only charged if you proceed after valuation (varies by asset type)
  • Administration Fee: One-time fee upon loan acceptance (disclosed upfront)
  • Initiation Fee: Regulated fee as per NCA guidelines (if applicable)
  • Monthly Service Fee: Small monthly account management fee

Transparent Disclosure:

  • All fees disclosed in writing before you commit
  • No hidden charges or surprise fees
  • Clear breakdown of all costs
  • Opportunity to ask questions about any fees

Fee Examples:

  • Vehicle Valuation: R500-R1,500 (depending on vehicle)
  • Property Valuation: R1,500-R3,500 (depending on property)
  • Administration Fee: Typically 1-2% of loan amount
  • Monthly Service Fee: R50-R150 per month

What's Included in Fees:

  • Professional asset valuation
  • Document preparation and processing
  • Credit assessment and risk analysis
  • Legal documentation and registration
  • Ongoing account management
  • Customer support throughout loan term

No Fee If Not Approved:

  • If we decline your application, there are no fees
  • If you decide not to proceed after valuation, only valuation fee applies
  • No penalty for withdrawing application

We're committed to fair, transparent pricing. All fees are discussed and agreed before any commitment is made.
