Luxury Asset Eligibility Requirements

Authentication

Items must be authentic with proof of purchase or certification where applicable.

Condition

Good condition with minimal wear. Original boxes and papers add value.

Market Value

Items must have established market value and demand.

Ownership

Clear proof of ownership required for all luxury items.

Documents Required

To process your luxury asset loan application, we'll need the following documentation:

Proof of Purchase

Original receipts, invoices, or purchase documentation showing ownership history.

Certification Papers

Gemological certificates, watch papers, art authentication certificates, or appraisal documents.

Owner Identification

Valid South African ID or passport and proof of residence (not older than 3 months).

Quality Photographs

Clear photographs showing all angles, markings, serial numbers, and any imperfections.
