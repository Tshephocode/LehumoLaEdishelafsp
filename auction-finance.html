What is Auction Finance?

Fast, short-term finance specifically designed for property auction purchases

Your Key to Auction Success

Auction finance is specialized bridging finance that allows you to bid with confidence at property auctions. Unlike traditional property purchases, auctions require:

  • Immediate payment - Usually within 24-72 hours
  • No finance conditions - Bids must be unconditional
  • Quick decisions - No time for traditional bank processes
  • Certain funding - Proof of funds required before bidding
  • Flexible terms - Bridge to long-term finance after purchase

Our auction finance solutions are designed specifically for the fast-paced auction environment, giving you the competitive edge you need.

How Auction Finance Works

Our streamlined process for auction property purchases

1

Pre-Auction Preparation

Contact us before the auction. We assess the property, provide a maximum bid limit, and pre-approve your finance. You receive a conditional approval letter to show auctioneers.

2

Bid with Confidence

Attend the auction knowing your finance is pre-approved. Bid up to your approved limit. Once you win, you sign the auction contract and pay the required deposit.

3

Fast Documentation

Submit the signed auction contract and deposit proof. We complete final checks and prepare loan documents. This typically happens within 24 hours of auction success.

4

Immediate Funding

We transfer the balance to the auctioneer within the required timeframe (usually 24-72 hours). You take possession of the property and can begin renovations or arrange long-term finance.
