General Questions

What is Lehumo La Edishela and what services do you offer? +

Lehumo La Edishela is a registered financial services provider specializing in asset-backed loans and bridging finance. We offer:

  • Asset-backed loans using vehicles, luxury items, property, and equipment as security
  • Bridging finance for property purchases, renovations, and developments
  • Commercial fleet financing for businesses
  • Fast approval loans with same-day decisions in many cases
  • Flexible repayment terms tailored to your needs

We're FSCA registered and have helped thousands of clients access quick funding using their assets as security.

Are you a registered financial services provider? +

Yes, Lehumo La Edishela is fully registered with the Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA). We operate in compliance with all South African financial regulations and consumer protection laws.

Our registration ensures:

  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Fair lending practices
  • Professional conduct standards
  • Client protection under South African law
  • Regular compliance monitoring

You can verify our registration status with the FSCA for your peace of mind.

How are you different from traditional banks? +

We offer several key advantages over traditional banks:

  • Speed: Approvals in hours vs. weeks at banks
  • Flexibility: We focus on your asset's value, not just credit scores
  • Accessibility: More types of assets accepted as security
  • Personal service: Direct access to decision-makers
  • Specialized expertise: Deep understanding of asset valuation
  • Innovative solutions: Tailored financing for unique situations

While banks often decline applications based on credit scores or strict criteria, we look at the intrinsic value of your assets and your ability to repay the loan.

What areas in South Africa do you serve? +

We provide services nationwide across South Africa. Our main office is in Sandton, Johannesburg, but we serve clients in:

  • Gauteng: Johannesburg, Pretoria, Midrand, Centurion
  • Western Cape: Cape Town, Stellenbosch, Paarl
  • KwaZulu-Natal: Durban, Pietermaritzburg
  • Eastern Cape: Port Elizabeth, East London
  • Free State: Bloemfontein
  • Mpumalanga: Nelspruit, Middelburg
  • All other provinces and major cities

We conduct remote valuations where possible and can arrange for asset collection/assessment anywhere in the country. Most of our application process can be completed online or over the phone.

Asset-Backed Loan Questions

What types of assets can I use as security for a loan? +

We accept a wide range of valuable assets as security for loans:

  • Vehicles: Cars, trucks, bakkies, motorcycles, caravans, boats
  • Luxury items: Watches, jewelry, diamonds, luxury handbags
  • Property: Residential, commercial, industrial, land
  • Equipment: Farming, construction, manufacturing, medical equipment
  • Collectibles: Art, antiques, rare coins, stamps, memorabilia
  • Electronics: High-end cameras, professional audio/video equipment
  • Fleet vehicles: Multiple vehicles for commercial operations

The asset must have clear ownership documentation and sufficient market value. We conduct professional valuations for all assets.

Can I still use my vehicle while it's securing a loan? +

Yes, in most cases you can continue using your vehicle while it secures your loan. We have two options:

  • Keep-and-Use Option: You keep the vehicle and continue using it normally. We register a notarial bond against the vehicle as security.
  • Secure Storage Option: For higher loan amounts or specific situations, we may store the vehicle in our secure facility.

Conditions for keeping your vehicle:

  • Maintain comprehensive insurance with us noted as interested party
  • Keep the vehicle in good condition
  • No modifications without our approval
  • Regular mileage updates may be required

This is one of our most popular features - getting cash without losing use of your vehicle.

How do you determine the value of my asset?

We use professional valuation methods tailored to each asset type:

  • Vehicles: Based on make, model, year, condition, mileage, service history, market demand, and current market prices for similar assets.
  • Luxury items: Expert appraisal by specialists, considering brand, model, age, condition, rarity, and current market values.
  • Property: Market valuation considering location, size, condition, comparable sales, and market trends.
  • Equipment: Replacement value, age, condition, maintenance records, and market demand.

Our valuation process:

  1. Initial assessment based on information provided
  2. Physical inspection by our experts (or detailed photos/videos)
  3. Market research and comparative analysis
  4. Professional valuation report
  5. Transparent explanation of the valuation

We aim to provide fair market valuations that reflect what your asset is truly worth.

What percentage of my asset's value can I borrow? +

Loan-to-value ratios vary by asset type, but generally:

  • Vehicles: Up to 80% of valuation
  • Luxury watches/jewelry: Up to 70% of valuation
  • Property: Up to 70% of valuation
  • Equipment: Up to 60% of valuation
  • Collectibles/art: Up to 50% of valuation

Factors affecting loan amount:

  • Asset type and liquidity
  • Asset condition and age
  • Market demand for the asset
  • Your repayment capacity
  • Loan term requested

We provide maximum flexibility while maintaining responsible lending practices. During the application process, we'll give you a clear indication of how much you can borrow against your specific asset.

Application & Process Questions

How long does the application process take? +

Our process is designed for speed:

  • Initial inquiry: 5-10 minutes online or by phone
  • Preliminary approval: 1-4 hours in most cases
  • Asset valuation: 24-48 hours (can be same-day for urgent cases)
  • Final approval: Within 24 hours of valuation
  • Funding: Same day or next business day after approval

Urgent applications: We offer expedited processing for urgent needs. With all documents ready and quick asset assessment, we can sometimes complete the entire process in under 24 hours.

Standard timeline: Most clients receive funds within 2-3 business days from initial application.

The speed depends on how quickly you can provide required documents and arrange asset assessment.

What documents do I need to apply? +

Basic requirements for all applicants:

  • South African ID document or passport
  • Proof of residence (not older than 3 months)
  • Recent bank statements (3 months)
  • Proof of income (payslips or financial statements)

Asset-specific documents:

  • Vehicles: Registration papers, license disc, service history
  • Property: Title deed, municipal account, rates clearance
  • Luxury items: Original purchase receipts, certificates, boxes
  • Equipment: Purchase invoices, registration documents

Business applicants (if applicable):

  • Company registration documents
  • Financial statements
  • Tax clearance certificate
  • Proof of business address

Don't worry if you're missing some documents - contact us and we'll advise on alternatives. We make the process as simple as possible.

Do you check credit scores? +

We conduct credit checks as part of our responsible lending practices, but we take a more holistic approach than traditional banks:

  • Credit check: Yes, we check credit history as part of affordability assessment
  • Not the only factor: Poor credit doesn't automatically mean rejection
  • Focus on assets: The primary security is your asset's value
  • Affordability assessment: We ensure you can comfortably repay the loan
  • Holistic view: We consider your entire financial picture

If you have poor credit:

  • We may still approve if the asset value is sufficient
  • We might adjust loan terms or amounts
  • We consider reasons for past credit issues
  • Current financial stability is more important than past problems

We believe in second chances and helping people rebuild their financial standing. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.

Can I apply if I'm self-employed or a business owner? +

Absolutely! We welcome self-employed individuals and business owners. We understand that traditional banks often make it difficult for entrepreneurs to access funding.

Requirements for self-employed applicants:

  • 6+ months of bank statements
  • Financial statements (if available)
  • Tax returns or SARS documents
  • Proof of business registration
  • Asset documentation

Special considerations for business owners:

  • We understand fluctuating income patterns
  • We consider business assets as well as personal assets
  • We offer business loans for commercial purposes
  • We can structure loans around business cash flow

Common scenarios we help with:

  • Business expansion funding
  • Equipment purchases
  • Working capital needs
  • Tax payment assistance
  • Opportunity funding

Our team has extensive experience working with entrepreneurs and understanding business financials.
