Collectible Eligibility Criteria

Authentication

Items must be authentic with proper documentation or expert verification.

Provenance

Clear ownership history and documentation of previous sales or exhibitions.

Condition

Good condition appropriate for age and type of collectible.

Market Value

Established auction records or comparable market sales.

Art & Collectible Documentation

Proper documentation is essential for accurate valuation of art and collectibles. We work with you to gather all necessary paperwork.

Required Documentation

Authentication Certificates

Certificates from recognized experts, galleries, or authentication services.

Provenance Documentation

Documented history of ownership, exhibition records, and publication history.

Purchase Documentation

Original purchase receipts, auction house invoices, or gallery paperwork.

Professional Photographs

High-quality photographs showing front, back, signatures, and any markings.

Condition Reports

Professional condition reports detailing any restoration or damage.
