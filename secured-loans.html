What Are Secured Loans?

A secured loan is a loan backed by an asset you own – your vehicle, property, luxury items, or equipment. This collateral gives you access to:

  • Lower interest rates than unsecured loans
  • Higher loan amounts based on your asset's value
  • Flexible repayment terms from 3 to 60 months
  • Keep using your asset while repaying the loan
  • Build or rebuild credit with regular payments
