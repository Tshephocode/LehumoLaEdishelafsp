What is Renovation Finance?

Short-term finance specifically designed for property improvements and renovations

Increase Property Value with Smart Financing

Renovation finance is specialized bridging finance that allows you to fund property improvements, increase value, and create your ideal living or investment space. Perfect for:

  • Homeowners - Improve your primary residence
  • Property Investors - Flip properties for profit
  • Landlords - Upgrade rental properties
  • Developers - Fund renovation projects
  • First-time Buyers - Fix up starter homes

Unlike traditional home improvement loans, our renovation finance focuses on the end value of your property and the potential return on investment from your renovations.

How Renovation Finance Works

Our simple 4-step process to fund your property improvements

1

Plan Your Project

Share your renovation plans, quotes from contractors, and property details with us. We assess the potential value increase.

2

Get Pre-Approved

Receive pre-approval within 24-48 hours based on current property value and renovation potential.

3

Access Funds

Funds are made available for your renovation project. We can pay contractors directly or provide staged payments.

4

Complete & Repay

Complete renovations, then either refinance to a long-term mortgage or sell the property to repay the loan.
