Our Simple 5-Step Process

We've streamlined our application process to make getting cash from your assets as easy as possible

1

Initial Inquiry & Assessment

Contact us via phone, online form, or WhatsApp. We'll ask a few basic questions about your asset and needs.

  • No commitment or credit check
  • Preliminary eligibility assessment
  • Get initial guidance on loan amounts
  • Discuss your specific requirements

Time: 10-15 minutes

2

Document Submission & Asset Valuation

Submit your documents and we'll arrange for professional valuation of your asset.

  • Submit required documentation
  • Professional asset valuation
  • Condition assessment
  • Market value determination

Time: 1-2 business days

3

Credit Assessment & Approval

Our credit team reviews your application and makes a lending decision.

  • Credit bureau check
  • Income verification
  • Affordability assessment
  • Final loan terms determined

Time: 2-4 hours (often same-day)

4

Contract Signing & Security Registration

Review and sign your loan agreement, and we register security if required.

  • Review loan agreement terms
  • Sign contracts electronically or in person
  • Register security (if applicable)
  • Final verification checks

Time: 1-2 business days

5

Funding & Aftercare

Receive your funds and begin your loan relationship with ongoing support.

  • Funds transferred to your account
  • Receive welcome pack
  • Set up repayments
  • Ongoing customer support

Time: Same day as approval (often)

Detailed Process Breakdown

1

Initial Inquiry & Assessment

Contact Methods

Choose how you want to contact us: phone call, online application form, WhatsApp message, or email. All methods receive the same priority attention.

Initial Discussion

We'll ask about: the asset you want to use, its approximate value, how much cash you need, your repayment preferences, and your basic contact information.

Preliminary Assessment

Based on our discussion, we'll give you initial guidance on whether your asset might qualify, approximate loan amounts possible, and next steps.

2

Document Submission & Asset Valuation

Document Collection

We'll provide you with a complete list of required documents based on your asset type. You can submit documents via email, WhatsApp, or our secure portal.

Asset Valuation Process

Our valuation experts assess your asset's current market value. For vehicles: physical inspection or detailed photo assessment. For luxury items: expert appraisal.

Verification Checks

We verify ownership documents, check for any encumbrances, and confirm the asset's condition meets our requirements.

Valuation Methods:
  • Vehicles: Physical inspection or detailed photo assessment with market comparisons
  • Luxury Items: Expert appraisal based on brand, condition, and market demand
  • Property: Professional valuation or comparative market analysis
  • Equipment: Depreciated cost assessment and market value determination
3

Credit Assessment & Approval

Credit Bureau Check

We perform a credit bureau check to assess your credit history. This is a standard part of responsible lending practices.

Affordability Assessment

We analyze your income and expenses to ensure the loan repayments are affordable and sustainable for your financial situation.

Final Decision

Our credit committee reviews all information and makes the final lending decision. Most applications are approved within hours.

Important Notes:
  • We consider applications from various credit backgrounds
  • Asset value plays a significant role in our assessment
  • We work with applicants to find suitable solutions
  • All decisions comply with NCA regulations
4

Contract Signing & Security Registration

Loan Agreement Review

We provide you with the complete loan agreement including all terms, conditions, fees, and repayment schedule. Take your time to review it carefully.

Signing Process

Sign the agreement electronically or in person at our office. We explain all terms and answer any questions you have before signing.

Security Registration

For certain assets (like vehicles or property), we register appropriate security interests to protect both parties. This is standard practice for secured loans.

Before You Sign:
  • Read all terms and conditions carefully
  • Ask questions about anything you don't understand
  • Ensure all personal details are correct
  • Verify the loan amount, interest rate, and repayment schedule
  • Check all fees and charges are as discussed
5

Funding & Aftercare

Funds Transfer

Once all documentation is complete, we transfer the funds directly to your bank account. Most clients receive funds on the same day as approval.

Welcome & Support

You'll receive a welcome pack with all loan details and contact information for our support team. We're here to help throughout your loan term.

Repayment Setup

We help you set up convenient repayment methods and provide payment reminders to ensure smooth loan management.
