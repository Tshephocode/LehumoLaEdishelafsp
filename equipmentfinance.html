Equipment Eligibility Criteria

Working Condition

Equipment must be in good working order with proper maintenance records.

Clear Ownership

Equipment must be fully paid for with clear proof of ownership.

Market Value

Equipment must have established resale value in the South African market.

Age Considerations

Generally, equipment should be less than 10 years old, depending on type.

Equipment Documentation Requirements

Proper documentation ensures accurate valuation and smooth processing. We help businesses gather all necessary paperwork.

Required Equipment Documentation

Proof of Purchase

Original purchase invoice or receipt showing equipment details and purchase price.

Registration Documents

Equipment registration papers, serial numbers, and model identification.

Maintenance Records

Service history, maintenance logs, and repair records showing proper care.

Business Documentation

Company registration, director IDs, and proof of business address.

Equipment Photographs

Clear photographs showing equipment condition, serial numbers, and current state.
